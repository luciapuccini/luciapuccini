A bit about me

As a highly experienced software developer with a focus on front-end development using React and Next.js, I have developed a deep understanding of the latest technologies and best practices. Over the past five years, I have worked with a range of startups, helping to bring their products to market and drive their success. I am currently working at Gartner, and my passion for front-end development drives me to constantly learn and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies. I look forward to discussing my experience and qualifications in more detail and learning more about how my skills can contribute to your team's success.
